## 2/ What it is
> Not a fiat-backed stablecoin. Not "guaranteed $1." An anti-inflationary token with an on-chain mechanism that pushes price toward $1 — strongly from above, partially from below. Here's exactly how. 👇

---

## 3/ The ceiling
> Above $1: anyone can buy TOBE from the protocol vault at exactly $1 and sell on the open market. That arbitrage caps price near $1. The vault holds 50% of every mint — this side is deep.

---

## 4/ The floor
> Below $1: anyone can sell TOBE to the vault at $1, drawn from an on-chain SOL reserve that grows every round. Real, but bounded — it defends as far as the reserve reaches, not infinitely. We say that plainly.

---

## 5/ The numbers
> 1,024 mint rounds. 10 SOL each. Round 1 mints 524,288 TOBE; every round after mints fewer, down to 512 in round 1,024. 50% to the minter, 50% to the protocol vault. No presale.

---

## 6/ Team allocation — disclosed
> The team wallet gets 8 free mint rounds (of 1,024 — under 1%), same 50/50 split as everyone, hard-capped in code, no setter to raise it. Round 9 on, the team pays the same 10 SOL as you.

---

## 7/ Founder revenue — disclosed
> When TOBE trades ≥$1, arbitrage-vault proceeds split 50/50 — half to the DAO treasury, half to a founder wallet. Only exists when TOBE trades above peg. No fee on minting, no fee on regular trading.
